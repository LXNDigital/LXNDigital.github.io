AI Governance


Strategic Executive Self-Assessment

Executive-level tool evaluating governance integration across board oversight, risk, policy, culture, and operations.

  • Ideal For: Executives seeking initial AI governance health check.
  • Outcome: Governance readiness overview with clarity on integration gaps.

Risk-Based Executive Self-Assessment

NIST AI RMF aligned assessment identifying AI risk exposure across six domains.

  • Ideal For: Senior leaders looking to detect governance blind spots early.
  • Outcome: Visibility of risk areas and prioritized mitigation strategies.

Third-Party Assessments

Evaluation of governance risks when using external AI services.

  • Ideal For: Enterprises using third-party AI in critical workflows.
  • Outcome: Third-party risk profile with confidence in vendor alignment.

Risk Diagnostic Service

Short-form diagnostic applying our business architecture model to highlight key risks and prioritise practical action.

  • Ideal For: Organisations responding to regulatory or expanding AI use.
  • Outcome: Risk-mapped maturity profile with practical remediation actions.

AI Governance Framework Design

AI governance isn’t a new silo—it’s a direct extension of your corporate governance framework. By applying our full business architecture model, we embed AI oversight alongside financial, cyber, and risk governance, making it integral to strategy, accountability, and board leadership.

  • Ideal for: Enterprises ready to make AI governance part of their organisational DNA.
  • Outcome: Cohesive, embedded AI governance—built to scale responsibly.
